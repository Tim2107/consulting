# Client Contract Compatibility Instructions

These instructions support AI governance without submitting client contracts to AI tools.

## Rule

Client contracts must not be uploaded, pasted, summarized, or otherwise submitted to AI tools unless an approved policy explicitly permits that use.

## Human-Led Check

For proposed AI workflows involving Client Confidential Data, a qualified reviewer should check whether the relevant client contract, statement of work, data-processing terms, security appendix, or confidentiality obligations restrict:

- outsourcing or subcontracting
- cloud processing
- cross-border transfers
- external provider access
- use of production data
- use of data for model training or improvement
- automated processing or automated decision support
- audit, logging, retention, or deletion requirements
- client notice or approval requirements

## Output

The reviewer should record only the result in the AI Use Case Intake:

- compatible
- compatible with conditions
- not compatible
- unclear, legal review required

Do not include contract excerpts unless policy later permits it.
