# Red Lines And Gated Exceptions

## Policy Intent

Red Lines define AI uses that are prohibited unless the board explicitly approves an exception. Gated Exceptions define restricted AI uses that may proceed only after specified gates are satisfied.

## Red Lines

- No Client Confidential Data in consumer AI tools.
- No client contracts submitted to AI tools unless an approved policy explicitly permits it.
- No training or fine-tuning on client material without explicit board, legal, and client approval.
- No secrets, credentials, keys, or production tokens in AI tools.
- No grey-market API proxies or unofficial AI gateways.
- No unapproved browser extensions or desktop agents with access to work systems.

## Gated Exceptions

- Automated deployment, release, and infrastructure action may proceed only as Technically Gated Action.
- AI-assisted changes to Delivery Pipeline Systems require scoped permissions, review gates, logs, and rollback.
- AI use with Client Confidential Data requires approved provider stacks and client-contract compatibility checks.
- Self-hosted or managed private deployments require approved operational ownership and security controls.
