## Board-Level Takeaways

- Bedrock, Azure OpenAI/Foundry, Vertex AI, xAI regional enterprise API, and self-hosted RAG are the main candidate routes for restricted sensitive workflows.
- Consumer tools and free developer tiers are not suitable for Client Confidential Data.
- Copilot, Gemini Workspace/Enterprise, Grok Business/Enterprise, and ChatGPT Enterprise are productivity/workspace routes; connector, agent, retention, and employee-transparency controls decide whether they can move beyond internal data.
- Self-hosting helps most when the board wants Client Confidential Data to remain inside a company or client-controlled boundary, but it creates operational ownership cost.
- Training or fine-tuning on company/client material remains a separate red-line exception.
