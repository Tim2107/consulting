# Adoption Phases

These phases translate governance decisions into a staged AI adoption path.

## Phase 0: Prohibited Until Decided

- No Client Confidential Data in unapproved AI tools.
- No training or fine-tuning on client material.

## Phase 1: Low-Risk Productivity

- Public or non-confidential internal data only.
- Approved tools and user guidance required.

## Phase 2: Controlled Internal Workflows

- Company data in approved enterprise tools.
- Logging, retention, access controls, and usage guidance required.

## Phase 3: Restricted Client Confidential Data Workflows

- Client Confidential Data only in approved provider stacks.
- Explicit routing constraints, legal review, audit controls, and client-contract compatibility required.

## Phase 4: High-Assurance Deployments

- Managed Private Deployment or Company-Operated Self-Hosted Option for sensitive scenarios.
- Hardware, operational ownership, security, monitoring, and support requirements must be approved.

## Phase 5: Training Or Fine-Tuning On Protected Material

- Exceptional path only.
- Requires explicit legal, client, and board approval.
